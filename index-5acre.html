Equestrian Estates at White Hills
5-Acre Ranch Lots Available from $59,900

Overview: What are the details about your FIVE ACRE land parcel?

  • Equestrian Estates at White Hills is the new subdivision of White Hills Land Development LLC. These lots are in a coveted area, just minutes from Lake Mead and Lake Mohave. The area is surrounded by BLM land, and is the closest deeded land to Lake Mead in Arizona.
  • Water is provided to each lot. Water is supplied via the neighborhood's privately owned water company. Private wells are allowed, if you so desire.
  • Electrical power and telephone lines are available. Some survivalists and self-sufficiency enthusiasts prefer to generate their own electricity or use solar power; these are welcome alternatives.
  • Roads to each home site are maintained by the County.
  • Horses welcome! Of course horse priviliges are included.
  • Great hiking & equestrian trails abound in the area. It's in a virtual forest of Joshua trees, and wild horses are often observed roaming the area. (Click the photo, above right, for a slideshow of wild horses roaming our property!)
  • Fishing and boating are both available within a few minutes' drive at either Lake Mead or Lake Mohave. There are marinas available at both lakes. Additionally, the Lake Mead Recreation area gives you access to thousands of acres of pristine land for horse riding, hiking and more.
  • It's only an approximate 45 minute commute to Las Vegas, 20 minutes to Boulder City, or 45 minutes to Kingman. You're far enough away to feel the privacy and solitude your soul craves, yet close enough to commute to a job or shopping.
  • Sensible protective covenants. No ridiculous "Stepford Wifes" rules set by an HOA far removed from reality.
  • Calm your senses with stunning sunsets and mountain views. At the same time, feel the peace and self fulfillment that comes with self sufficiency.
